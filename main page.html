Goverment Debt

This project is aimed to help young people understanding the amount of debt the goverment digs itself into evry year and how it is difficult
to stay out of debt. A quick guide to this game, you have four options on how much to spend on each department the more you spend the more chance
you have of going into debt, and the main objective is to stay out of debt.
